FAQ

Questions we always get asked

What size of businesses do you work with? +
We work with startups, SMEs, and large enterprises across India. Our engagement models are flexible enough to fit any scale.
How long does onboarding typically take? +
For standard engagements, onboarding takes 2–4 weeks. Complex multi-service setups may take 6–8 weeks. We prioritise zero disruption to your existing workflows.
Do you offer customised pricing? +
Yes. Every proposal is customised based on your service requirements, volume, and preferred engagement type — project, retainer, or embedded team.
Which cloud platforms do you support? +
We support AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform. Our architects are certified across all three and can design multi-cloud or hybrid solutions as needed.
Is my data secure with Renviko? +
Absolutely. We follow strict data handling protocols, NDAs are signed at engagement commencement, and all cloud services comply with ISO 27001 standards.
Can I start with one service and expand? +
Yes — and many clients do exactly this. You can start with accounts outsourcing and expand into logistics and SCM as the relationship grows.
Do you handle GST and Indian compliance? +
Yes. Our finance team is well-versed in GST, TDS, ROC filings, and all Indian regulatory requirements. Always audit-ready.
What reporting do clients get? +
All clients get access to a dedicated dashboard with real-time reporting. Weekly summaries, monthly reviews, and quarterly business reviews are included in every engagement.
